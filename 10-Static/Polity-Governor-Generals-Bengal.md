# List of Governor Generals of Bengal (1773–1833)

This office was created by the [[Regulating Act of 1773]], which converted the 'Governor of Bengal' into the **'Governor-General of Bengal'**.

## Key Individuals

### 1. [[Warren Hastings]] (1772–1785)
- First Governor-General of Bengal.
- Ended the Dual System of government.
- Involved in [[Regulating Act of 1773]] and [[Pitt’s India Act of 1784]].

### 2. [[Lord Cornwallis]] (1786–1793)
- Introduced the **Permanent Settlement** of Bengal (1793).
- Known as the **'Father of Civil Services in India'**.

### 3. [[Lord Wellesley]] (1798–1805)
- Introduced the **Subsidiary Alliance** system.

### 4. [[Lord Hastings]] (1813–1823)
- Policy of 'Paramountcy'.

### 5. [[Lord William Bentinck]] (1828–1833)
- Last Governor-General of Bengal.
- Became the first [[Governor General of India]] via the [[Charter Act of 1833]].
